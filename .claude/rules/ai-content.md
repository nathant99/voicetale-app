# AI Content Accuracy Policy

For apps using FoundationModels or any AI to generate educational content:

1. **All AI-generated content is draft until verified** — Never ship unreviewed AI output
2. **Known risk areas** — English spelling/grammar rules (never state absolutes), science facts with specific numbers, math worked examples, historical dates, health/safety information
3. **Hedging language** — When uncertain about a rule, use "usually", "often", "in most cases"
4. **Bug reports about content accuracy are P0 priority** — After fixing, search for the same pattern across all content files
